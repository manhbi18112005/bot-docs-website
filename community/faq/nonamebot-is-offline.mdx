First make sure the bot is offline!

![Bot is indicating an online status](./assets/bot-online.png)

If he is indeed offline, you can join our [support server](https://bot.nonamestudio.xyz/discord) and check the last messages in #announcements and #bot-status.

:::tip 
In the event of bot being offline and we are aware there is nothing you can do, just be patient whilst we wake him up!
:::

:::caution
If we do not seem aware of the issue, you can let us know by pinging or DM a support.
:::
